About the Tzu Chi Youth Association

Our Mission

The Tzu Chi Youth Association (TCYA) is dedicated to fostering unity, leadership, and compassion among middle and high school students (ages 12 to 18) nationwide. By organizing impactful local, national, and international events, we aim to instill our core values while promoting humanitarian culture, community service, and environmental stewardship.

Our Vision

To establish a cohesive network of young leaders empowered to effect positive change within their communities and beyond through initiatives that inspire, educate, and unite.

Leadership & Growth

TCYA operates with a dedicated Core Team structure that provides practical project management and leadership experience for our youth. From planning national retreats to executing nationwide community service campaigns, our students take the reins, cultivating the next generation of civic-minded leaders.
