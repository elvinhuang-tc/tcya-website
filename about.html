About the Tzu Chi Youth Association

Our Mission

The Tzu Chi Youth Association (TCYA) empowers young people ages 12 to 18 to become active, compassionate contributors to their communities. Through service-driven education, environmental stewardship, and humanitarian culture, we help students develop the skills, values, and confidence to lead with integrity.

Our Vision

To establish a cohesive network of young leaders empowered to effect positive change within their communities and beyond through initiatives that inspire, educate, and unite.

Our Core Commitments

  • Inspire youth to serve others and the environment with sincerity.
  • Create supportive opportunities for learning, growth, and reflection.
  • Build inclusive programs that bring students together in meaningful service.
  • Encourage a mindset of kindness, gratitude, and responsible action.

Why Support TCYA

Support for TCYA enables a scalable youth movement grounded in compassion and service. Grants and partnerships help us expand access to high-impact programs, strengthen volunteer capacity, and ensure more young people can grow as effective, values-driven leaders.
