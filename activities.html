Signature Programs & Activities

Annual Youth Leadership Retreat

National Youth Retreat

Focus: Environmental Protection & Leadership

A 4-day intensive program bringing youth together from across the United States. Through workshops, classes, and team-building activities, participants explore how to strengthen their local communities through environmental stewardship and collaborative leadership.

Global Service Learning

Taiwan Service Learning Trip

Focus: Global Citizenship & Humanitarian Aid

A 12-day international learning experience. Students participate in hands-on service, teach English at local schools, engage in cultural exchanges, and learn the logistics of global humanitarian missions. This trip builds lifelong friendships and a deepened worldview.

National Service Month

Nationwide Community Action

Focus: Local Impact, United Effort

To demonstrate the unity and shared mission of chapters across the country, TCYA coordinates national service months. During these periods, every chapter designs and executes a project tailored to their local community's specific needs—from nursing home visits and food banks to youth mentoring and shelter support.
