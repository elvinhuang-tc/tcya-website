Prohibited Items

Possession of these items may result in immediate dismissal from the program and being sent home early at your family's expense:

  • Firearms, weapons, or knives
  • Drugs, alcohol, tobacco, or cigarettes
  • Vapes, vape pens, or other e-cigarettes
  • Explicit, violent, or inappropriate materials

Please use common sense to ensure everything you bring is appropriate for this trip.
