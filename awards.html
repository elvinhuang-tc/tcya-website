Youth Volunteer Service Awards

Recognizing the tangible contributions and holistic dedication of our youth volunteers.

Foundation of Compassion Award

Recognizes overall volunteer commitment through a tally of total volunteer hours within a single year.

  • Bronze: 50+ hours
  • Silver: 100+ hours
  • Gold: 150+ hours
  • Platinum: 200+ hours

Unified Mission Service Award

Encourages engagement across diverse service sectors. Applicants must meet hour requirements in their top three mission categories.

  • Bronze: 10+ hours per mission
  • Silver: 15+ hours per mission
  • Gold: 20+ hours per mission
  • Platinum: 25+ hours per mission

Our Four Core Service Missions

Charity

Disaster relief, food banks, homeless outreach, and winter distributions.

Education

Tutoring, youth leadership training, and environmental workshops.

Medicine

Medical outreach support, health education, and Red Cross collaborations.

Community Culture

Environmental cleanups, cultural celebrations, and community arts.
